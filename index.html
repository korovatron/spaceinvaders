Space Invaders - Classic Arcade Game

Experience the legendary Space Invaders arcade game right in your browser! This faithful JavaScript recreation of the 1978 classic lets you defend Earth from waves of descending alien invaders. Use your cannon to shoot down the alien fleet before they reach the bottom of the screen. Features include authentic retro graphics, classic sound effects, destructible shields, and bonus UFO targets. Perfect for retro gaming enthusiasts and anyone who wants to experience this pivotal piece of video game history.

This Space Invaders game requires HTML5 canvas support. Please upgrade to a modern browser to play this classic arcade game.
